nilenso is a fully remote, employee-owned co-operative software consultancy based in India and Canada with more than 10 years of experience building thoughtful, well-crafted products. And we’re hiring right now!

  • We place employee happiness first.
  • We strive to be a progressive organization, which is often years ahead of present policies.
  • We place a lot of trust in our employees to do the right thing.


A great place to work

  • We are a company filled with people who are kind, understanding and passionate about programming.
  • We care about collaboration, not internal competition.
  • We deeply care about our craft, and focus on personal and professional growth.
  • We believe in internal transparency and workplace democracy. Our salaries and annual reviews are open to everyone in the organization. We have no corporate secrets.

A track record for doing challenging work

  • We have solved challenging business problems, at scale for a host of organizations ranging from fast-moving start-ups, to stable fintechs, to global healthcare NGOs.
  • Historically, the majority of our projects have been staff-augmentation. While being a consultant, we own product/business outcomes, work deeply with the team, be on-call and participate in resolving production issues.
  • We see ourselves as insiders, not outsiders. We will work with or as a team-lead, and collaborate with product managers and stakeholders.

Work-life balance

  • We normalize working reasonable hours with breaks. Except to deal with the odd production issue, we don't work late evenings or on weekends.
  • Ensonians enjoy 33 days of paid leave annually.
  • We offer paid menstrual leave for anyone who needs it, no questions asked. This does not count as leave.
  • We believe that parenting is a joy and a responsibility that should be shared by all parents. Anyone (regardless of gender) welcoming a newborn (biological or adopted) is offered 6 months paid and a further 6 months unpaid leave.

Remote-friendly policies

  • We cover the basic requirements to make your work more enjoyable by providing a full workstation, which includes, but is not limited to:
    • A Mac/Linux machine.
    • A standing desk and a comfortable chair.
    • A monitor, mechanical keyboard, mouse, and webcam.
    • Best-in-class headphones.
    • If you need anything else, just ask, we'll try.
  • We offer a monthly co-working space budget of ₹ 25,000.
  • Ensonians like to collaborate in person. We hold annual company-wide retreats to foster in-person connections and team integration.

Growth and mentorship

  • Ensonians have a flexible expense account with an unlimited learning budget.
  • An extensive library (buy any book, no questions asked) and frequent conference attendance is our baseline.
  • Senior Ensonians care about mentoring and helping each-other grow.

Health and Well-being

  • Health insurance for you and your family of ₹ 6 lakhs per person.
  • nilenso also reimburses any expenses (consultation, therapy, medication and any other expenses relating to your mental well being) up to ₹ 1L/year for you, your partner, parents or children.
