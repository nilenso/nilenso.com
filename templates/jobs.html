come, run nilenso with us

We’re passionate, curious folk looking to work with passionate, curious folk.

At nilenso, we want to work on ideas that have the power to transform the world. Along the way, we're building a kind, nurturing community of people who care about doing things the “right way”, while learning and improving constantly.

As an employee-owned cooperative, we all are stakeholders in nilenso, and don't need to justify how happy employees impact the bottom line. Here, you have the unique opportunity to chart our own path in a stable environment that provides financial and emotional security.

We’re a growing team of friendly people across India with one member in Canada, and are hiring in both of these geographies. Last year amidst COVID-19, we became a fully remote company. We expect to continue to operate remotely in 2022.

Learn more about interviewing and working with us from our handbook.

essentials and perks

If you think companies should be run the way nilenso is, get in touch!

We are passionate about our work, but believe that balance is essential. We normalise working reasonable hours with breaks. We currently work a 4.5 day week, offer paid menstrual leave, as well as paid parental leave and additional paid time off.

We have an unlimited learning budget. An extensive library (buy any book, no questions asked) and frequent conference attendance are our baseline. We've sponsored and attended conferences all over the world: Clojure/conj, Euroclojure, Clojure/West, IN/Clojure, StrangeLoop, Functional Conf, FOSSMeet, RubyConf India, RubyConf San Diego, JSFoo, rootconf, React Conf SF, GCRC, Fifth Elephant, and probably more that we've forgotten.

We believe in transparency: our finances, salaries and reviews are fully open. We have no corporate secrets.

We are keen on hearing from women and other under-represented communities.
